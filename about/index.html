Background

I was born and raised in Ghana, a beautiful country in West Africa, with one older sister, my mother and father. After completing my high school education at SOS Hermann Gmeiner International College in 2014, I moved to the USA for college. I initially started out as an Architecture and switched to Information Technology and Management after a year. In my opinion, this was one of the best decisions I've ever made. I am currently pursuing both my bachelors and masters in ITM with a specialization in Software Development and Data management respectively. I work as a freelance Graphic Designer in my free time.

Goals and Aspirations

After college, I hope to own my very own Graphics Design firm. I also hope to start a company that focuses on teaching young women in Ghana how to code. In terms of ITM, I am willing to go wherever the wind takes me.
